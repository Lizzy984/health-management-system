Quinoa Buddha Bowl

A nutrient-packed bowl with quinoa, roasted vegetables, and tahini dressing

Ingredients

  • 1 cup quinoa, rinsed
  • 2 cups water
  • 1 sweet potato, cubed
  • 1 red bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 cup broccoli florets
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 avocado, sliced
  • 2 tbsp tahini
  • 1 lemon, juiced
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Preparation

  1. Preheat oven to 400°F (200°C)
  2. Cook quinoa in water according to package instructions
  3. Toss sweet potato, bell pepper, and broccoli with olive oil and seasonings
  4. Roast vegetables for 20-25 minutes until tender
  5. Whisk together tahini, lemon juice, and water to make dressing
  6. Assemble bowls with quinoa, roasted vegetables, and avocado
  7. Drizzle with tahini dressing and serve

Mediterranean Stuffed Bell Peppers

Colorful bell peppers filled with a savory mixture of grains, vegetables, and herbs

Ingredients

  • 4 large bell peppers
  • 1 cup cooked brown rice
  • 1 can chickpeas, rinsed
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1/2 cup feta cheese, crumbled
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • Fresh parsley for garnish
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Preparation

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C)
  2. Cut tops off bell peppers and remove seeds
  3. In a bowl, mix rice, chickpeas, tomatoes, feta, and herbs
  4. Stuff mixture into bell peppers
  5. Place in baking dish and drizzle with olive oil
  6. Bake for 25-30 minutes until peppers are tender
  7. Garnish with fresh parsley and serve
